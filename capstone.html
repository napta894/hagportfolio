Lotus app is a digital solution for users that are ingredient-conscious as they often find it challenging to find a truly organic product and they lack time to check ingredients labels.
The app's main features are a scanner to check organic products ingredients and shopping online to find an organic products to compare with your own.




Role

UX/UI designer

Duration

10 Weeks

Tools

Figma

Invision

Project type

Academic Project



Problem space

Health and environmentally conscious people want to use cosmetic products that are truly organic. However, they find it very difficult to find such products as they can not understand and are often misled by organic cosmetic products labels.

Solution

An app that allow users to scan organic ingredients products, read quickly ingredients lists and find an organic product to compare with their own.

Design process

Method: Double diamond

Phase 1: Discovery

Secondary research

Primary research

In this step, I created questions to understand users' behaviour, motivation and pain points in terms of misleading brand experiences and how they interact when buying an organic product.

Hypothesis statement

I believe that healthy and ingredient-conscious people are misled when buying organic products I will know if I am right or wrong when I see the following feedback from the market: participants mistrusting labels and secondary data concerning mistrusting labels.




To confirm my hypothesis I interviewed 4 users from 20-55 that are
health and environmentally conscious. Here what they said:

“I do not believe brands when they say is natural because sometimes
people should be careful about allergens.”

“It is very difficult to find organic products”

“Sometimes I feel overwhelmed and confused
when I buy organic products online””

Key Findings

Difficulty trusting
brands

Users finds difficult to trust brands as “Greenwashing”
it is relatively common.

Online shopping experience

When buying online
users find it hard to explore organic product options

Transparent and descriptive Ingredient labels

Users find it difficult to read the ingredients labels and they do not have time to read them all.

Phase 2: Define

I considered all the findings and themes from the discovery phase
and analysed them to synthesize them into a more meaningful knowledgeable definition.

How might we help healthy and ingredient-conscious people better understand organic cosmetic products labels?





User stories

Based on our persona and experience mapping I developed 28 user stories and 3 epics.

Searching for organic cosmetic products, organic products label transparency and an enhanced online shopping experience.
