Project overview

Lotus app is a digital solution for users that are ingredient-conscious as they often find it challenging to find a truly organic product and they lack time to check ingredients labels.



Problem space

Health and environmentally conscious people want to use cosmetic products that are truly organic. However, they find it very difficult to find such products as they can not understand and are often misled by organic cosmetic products labels.

Goal

The goal is to help consumers feel confident that they have purchased truly organic products and help them better understand product labels.

Task flow selection

The selected task flow is searching for an organic cosmetic product because it is a crucial task for the persona.

Laura searches and selects a face moisturizer from the search result page. She reads the product's ingredients and
adds the product to the cart. She scans a product to compare it with the one added to the cart. Finally, she orders the discovered face moisturizer.

Usability testing

Two rounds of usability testing were conducted with 5 testers to improve the design and find usability issues.

Round 1

The tasks were simple to undertake, however, most of the testers were expecting more on the ingredient details as well on the scanning product page .
Main issues were going back arrow not visible, ingredient listing confusion and text readability

Round 2

Overall the tasks were completed by most of the users except for scanning an organic product.
Main issues where difficulty clicking on scanning icon
prototyping missed on product page and
ingredient lists.
