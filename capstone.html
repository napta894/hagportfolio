Lotus app is a digital solution for users that are ingredient-conscious as they often find it challenging to find a truly organic product and they lack time to check ingredients labels.
The app's main features combine a scanner to check organic products ingredients and shopping online to find an organic products to compare with your own.




Role

UX/UI designer

Duration

10 Weeks

Tools

Figma

Invision

Project type

Academic Project




Problem space

Health and environmentally conscious people want to use cosmetic products that are truly organic. However, they find it very difficult to find such products as they can not understand and are often misled by organic cosmetic products labels.

Problem space

Lotus app solves users' pain points in 3 ways:


  • Scanner to check ingredients labels
  • Detailed product page with categorized ingredient lists
  • Find an organic product that is certified and verified by Lotus.

Design process

Method: Double diamond

Phase 1: Empathize

In this step, I started researching deeper on the problem space where relevant findings are established following primary research and secondary research. I created questions to understand users' behaviour, motivation and pain points in terms of misleading brand experiences and how they interact when buying an organic product.
