Business Background

Wedvenue is a wedding venues preview digital agency in the form of a mobile application
Typically is an online search tool that can be downloaded either on android and Apple. WedVenue strives to deliver high quality location venues with available booking system and a filter feature.
We aim to assist engaged couples who finds uneasy to book a venue due to lack the time and limited booking availability.
