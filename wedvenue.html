Project Overview

Wedvenue is a wedding venues preview digital agency in the form of a mobile application
WedVenue strives to deliver high quality location venues with available booking system and a filter feature.
We aim to assist engaged couples who finds uneasy to book a venue due to lack the time and limited booking availability.

Competitive audit

I conducted a competitive audit to understand global markets between two most popular wedding services Weddingwire and Zola.
From this research I found out the best values propositions from competitors and their weakness.

Opportunities: Be more descriptive,Search tools fluidity, focus on audiences preferences.

Who: WeddingWire is the largest
and most trusted global marketplace connecting engaged couples with local wedding professionals.
Strenghts: Comprehensive tips on etiquette, planning, budgeting, and more.
Weakness: Useful wedding-planning checklists.

Who: The wedding company that'll do anything for love, is reinventing the wedding planning and registry experience with a free suite of planning tools.
Strenghts: Useful wedding-planning checklists
Weakness: So many choices can feel overwhelming.

Key findings

Round 1

The lists of venues need to be visible at the first page with indication of scrolling.

The search button must be visible and relevant.

The current booking design will stay the same.

Round 2

The back icons need to be replaced with a cross so they can remain where they were doing.

The request price button should be added at the request viewing page.
