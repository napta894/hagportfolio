Project Overview

Wedvenue is a wedding venue preview digital agency in the form of a mobile application
WedVenue strives to deliver high-quality location venues with available booking system and a filter feature.
We aim to assist engaged couples who find uneasy to book a venue due to a lack the time and limited booking availability.

The Problem

Target customers want a stress -free search of online app venues.
Low budgets, clear description, filter option
and booking availability within the time frame.

The goal

Create a quality wedding venues preview app with clear quality videos and ease of access regarding booking and description clarity. Simple and organized application.

Competitive audit

I conducted a competitive audit
to understand the global markets between the two most popular wedding
services Weddingwire and Zola.


Opportunities: Be more descriptive, Search tools fluidity, and focus on audiences preferences.

Who: WeddingWire is the largest
and most trusted global marketplace connecting engaged couples with local wedding professionals.
Strengths: Comprehensive tips on etiquette, planning, budgeting, and more.
Weakness: Useful wedding-planning checklists.

Who: The wedding company that'll do anything for love, is reinventing the wedding planning and registry experience with a a free suite of planning tools.
Strenghts: Useful wedding-planning checklists

Key findings

Users Needs

and venue descriptions should be in real-time.

Specific Venue Services

From the comparative analysis and the competitive audit ,

Major Issues

Round 1

The lists of venues need to be visible on the first page with an indication of scrolling.

The search button must be visible and relevant.

The current booking design will stay the same.

Round 2

The back icons need to be replaced with a cross so they can remain where they were doing.

There should be a button to request a price on the request viewing page.
